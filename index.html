Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Kwick

How does Kwick work? +

Kwick gives you a unique QR code that customers scan with their phone camera. They choose their payment method (Apple Pay, Google Pay, or card), enter the amount, and pay instantly. The money goes directly to your Kwick account, and you can transfer it to your bank anytime.

Do my customers need to download an app? +

No! That's the beauty of Kwick. Your customers just scan the QR code with their phone's camera and pay through their browser. No app downloads, no account creation required.

What are the fees? +

Kwick charges 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction, which is standard for payment processing. This covers the Stripe processing fees and our platform costs. There are no monthly fees, setup fees, or hidden charges.

How quickly do I get paid? +

Payments appear in your Kwick account instantly after the customer pays. You can transfer your balance to your bank account anytime, and transfers typically arrive in 1-2 business days.

Is it secure? +

Absolutely. All payments are processed through Stripe, the same payment processor used by companies like Amazon, Google, and Shopify. Your customers' payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.

Can I use Kwick for my business? +

Yes! Kwick works for anyone who needs to accept payments. Whether you're a freelancer, small business owner, service provider, or just collecting tips at your job, Kwick makes it easy.

What payment methods are supported? +

Kwick supports all major payment methods including Apple Pay, Google Pay, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), and debit cards.

Can I print my QR code? +

Yes! You can download your QR code and print it on business cards, table tents, receipts, or anywhere else you want to display it. It works just as well printed as it does on your phone screen.

Is there a minimum payment amount? +

The minimum payment is $1.00. There is no maximum limit, though individual card networks may have their own transaction limits.

How do I sign up? +

Click "Get Started" and you'll be guided through a quick signup process. You'll need to provide your email, phone number, and bank account details for payouts. The whole process takes about 2 minutes.
